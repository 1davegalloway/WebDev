Latitude vs. Cloudiness

A third observation is within the realm of cloudiness. There are many variables not accounted for in this dataset. For example: types of clouds, measurements over land or sea, seasonal changes, and limitations of accurate observations of cloudiness during the night. However, the reality seems to be that 0 to 10 percent cloud cover is common at nearly all latitudes. I'm interested to know if the common trends seen at 0-10% and 75-90% are identifiers of Spring and Fall (otherwise considered the "early" and "late" rains).
